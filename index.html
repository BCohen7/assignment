The Top 100 Golf Courses of Australia

Golf is one of the fastest growing sports in Australia, with it being popular quite consistently all across the country. Golf Travel is a huge market, and as destination golf increases as a business, knowing where the best courses are and which ones you'd like to visit becomes more and more pivotal. Golf Digests' 2024 rankings of best courses are visualised below, intending to not only be interesting, but to act as a tool for planning your next golf getaway

While in some countries (namely America) golf can be rather exclusive, in Australia we have the privlidge of premium golf still being accessible for the greater public. As seen on this population pyramid, multiple options at any level within the rankings are present for the playing public

Rankings are taken from Golf Digest Australia. Location data was gained from Google Maps, with all information on club status being collected from respective clubs websites.
